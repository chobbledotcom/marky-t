Hello there! I'm Mark, but you can call me Marky T - **Essex Wedding Award Finalist 2020** for Wedding DJ of the Year, Radio Essex presenter, and full-time professional DJ since 2002.

## My Journey

My love affair with music began when I was just 9 years old, equipped with my first set of disco lights and a twin tape deck with speakers. I spent as many weekends as possible DJing for family and friends, developing my skills through word-of-mouth recommendations.

In 2002, I made the decision to leave college and pursue DJing professionally - a choice I've never regretted. My first residency came in 2003 at the legendary Talk nightclub in Southend. Since then, I've worked at some of Essex's most iconic venues including:

- **Sugar Hut** (of TOWIE fame)
- **Dukes Genesis** (Chelmsford)
- **Mayhem** & **Chameleon**
- **Eclipse** & **Sky Bar**
- **Pop World** & **Bar Bluu**
- **Chicago's**, **Unit 7**, **Moo Moos**

These rooms taught me everything about reading a crowd, managing energy levels, and keeping a dance floor full - skills that directly translate to weddings and private events.

## On the Airwaves

My broadcasting career took off when I secured **second place in Capital FM's 'Radio Stars'** national competition. I've hosted daytime shows on Funky SX and currently present **weekday shows on Radio Essex**, Essex's major commercial radio station - sometimes covering the breakfast show alongside Martin Day and Su Harrison, reaching **over 100,000 listeners**.

As a radio presenter, I update my music library weekly - I get new tracks before the general public and know exactly what's being requested right now. No tired playlists from 2015 here.

## Major Events & Residencies

Beyond private bookings, I'm trusted to host Essex's biggest public events:

- **Resident DJ at Chelmsford City Racecourse** - Their official DJ for Fireworks Spectaculars, Christmas parties, and major events
- **Billericay Fireworks** - 15,000+ attendees annually, now in its 54th year, the biggest fireworks event in Essex
- **March Farm** events and community festivals
- Regular performances on stage with the Radio Essex team

When your wedding reception has 150 guests, know that I've kept 15,000 people entertained. I've got this.

## What I Bring

I read crowds, mix live, and create atmospheres that keep everyone dancing. Twenty-two years of professional experience means I've seen trends come and go and I know what works.

## Personal Life

When I'm not behind the decks, I'm a husband to my amazing wife and daddy to my two beautiful daughters. I'm living my dream every day, creating memories across Essex and the UK!

Want to make your special day truly unforgettable? Let's chat about how I can help craft the perfect soundtrack for your celebration!
